# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity.

## Our standards

**Positive behaviour:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behaviour:**
- Harassment or discriminatory language
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported by opening a GitHub issue or contacting the maintainers directly. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly.
